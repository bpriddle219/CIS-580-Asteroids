How To Play
Up Arrow/W: Thrust your ship forward
Left Arrow/A: Turn your ship to the left
Right Arrow/D: Turn your ship to the right
Space: Shoot lasers
Enter: Warp to a random location on screen, but watch out, you could warp into asteroids!

Destroy the asteroids to earn points
Watch out for stray UFOs
Lose all three lives and it's game over!
